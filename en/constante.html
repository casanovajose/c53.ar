2015
Type:
Video Installation
Author:
C53 (Colectivo 53)

Description

A video will be played in a loop from a 14" television. It will be placed on a wooden table facing a short distance from the vertex of two white walls. The emission of cathode rays and the saturation of the video colors generate an abstract projection on the wall, which is completed with the presence of the viewer when transiting the installation.
